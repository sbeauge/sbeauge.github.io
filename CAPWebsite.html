Welcome from the Sisters at UCF!

Welcome to the website of the Prevailing Psi Chapter of Delta Phi Omega Sorority, Incorporated at the University of Central Florida. You will find everything that you need to know about the largest, strongest and fastest growing South Asian interest sorority.

We encourage you to search our website to learn more about us and what we do for the Orlando and University of Central Florida community and why WE are Destined to Prevail Against All Odds.

Please feel free to contact any of our members through email or our executive board directly at dpo.ucf@gmail.com or visit our national website at deltaphiomega.org!

Thank you for your interest, we cannot wait to meet you!
